Crepes

Crepes are usually of two types: sweet crepes (crepes sucrees) and savoury galettes (crepes salees). Crepes are served with a variety of fillings, from the simplest with only sugar to flambeed crepes Suzette or elaborate savoury galettes. Crepes originate in Brittany, a region in the west of France.
